# One-page concept brief

## Audience / problem
Adults, caregivers, journalists, employers, and policy teams need a neutral way to inspect cannabis claims whose certainty exceeds their sources. Search results often blend research, advocacy, and commerce without exposing uncertainty.

## Positioning
Marijuana Fact Check is the citation-first evidence desk: exact claim in, transparent grade and source trail out. It is not a lifestyle brand, dispensary guide, or substitute for a clinician or attorney.

## Information architecture
Claim database → methodology → source standard → submission queue → correction log. Legal, privacy, and commercial disclosure are first-class routes.

## Visual direction
A newsroom/research-terminal system based on IBM Carbon principles: black masthead, white/gray evidence surfaces, one blue interactive color, sharp corners, visible grid, IBM Plex typography. Grade colors are semantic and always accompanied by text.

## Content pillars
1. Evidence records  2. Uncertainty explainers  3. Source notes  4. Material corrections  5. Monthly research brief.

## CTA / revenue hypothesis
Primary conversion: evidence-update signup. Secondary: claim submission. Revenue tests: paid research report and compliance-reviewed sponsorship inquiry. No dispensary affiliates or product CTAs in MVP.
