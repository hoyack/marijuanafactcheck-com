# Review handoff

## CMO
Verify locked title/H1, plain-language grades, no minors targeting, no medical advice/dosage/product promotion, screenshots, and sponsor labeling policy.

## CRO
Confirm ICP, `source:mfc`, `offer:mfc-media`, newsletter/report/sponsor conversions, and no dispensary affiliates.

## CTO
Review static architecture, CSP/headers, forms/honeypots, CI, dependency audit, source links, DNS preservation, production TLS, monitoring, form-delivery proof, and rollback. CTO owns merge and production cutover.

## Closure evidence
Approved PR; green CI; CMO/CRO acceptance; production URL; valid HTTPS; forms detected and delivered; analytics decision; preserved email DNS; monitoring and rollback proof.
