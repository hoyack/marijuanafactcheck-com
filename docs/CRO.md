# CRO contract

- ICP: evidence-seeking adults, caregivers, reporters, employers, and policy/compliance teams.
- Offer: a free monthly evidence update; future paid research report.
- Primary conversion: successful `mfc-newsletter` form submit.
- Secondary conversion: `mfc-claim` submission and sponsor inquiry email.
- CRM tags: `source:mfc`, `offer:mfc-media`.
- Revenue: labeled newsletter/research sponsorship and institutional report; never pay-to-grade.

## Intake ownership and SLA

FormSubmit delivers to `hello@hoyack.com`. Revenue Operations (Phenex) owns intake routing and CRM capture. First response target: one business day for newsletter/delivery exceptions and sponsor inquiries; claim submissions are acknowledged by the form and follow the editorial triage cadence. Never copy private medical information into CRM notes.

## 30 / 60 / 90

- 30 days: publish six seed records; invite correction and claim submissions; instrument privacy-minimized conversions.
- 60 days: publish two evidence updates and test an institutional report waitlist.
- 90 days: review subscriber quality, correction rate, sponsor fit, and whether a paid evidence brief is defensible. No ad or affiliate expansion without compliance approval.
