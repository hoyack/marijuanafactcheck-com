Privacy

Privacy notice

What we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with.

Data we collect

Newsletter subscriptions: When you subscribe to the evidence newsletter, we collect your email address and optionally your first name. This data is processed by FormSubmit and delivered to hello@hoyack.com.

Claim submissions: When you submit a claim for evaluation, we collect the claim text, source information, and optionally your email address and notes. This data is processed by FormSubmit.

Analytics: We use Plausible, a privacy-first analytics service that does not use cookies and does not collect personal data. Plausible collects aggregated page-view counts, referrer sources, and device types. No individual user profiles are created.

How we use your data

  • Email addresses submitted for the newsletter are used only to deliver the Marijuana Fact Check evidence newsletter.
  • Claim submissions are added to the public research queue.
  • We do not sell, rent, share, or trade your personal data with third parties.

Third-party services

FormSubmit: Newsletter and claim-submission forms are processed by FormSubmit. FormSubmit's privacy practices are available at formsubmit.co/privacy.

Plausible: Aggregate analytics are provided by Plausible. Plausible's data policy is available at plausible.io/data-policy.

Your rights

  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time via the link in each email.
  • Request deletion of your data by emailing hello@hoyack.com.
  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.

Data retention

Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Claim submission data is retained as part of the public research record.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact hello@hoyack.com.
