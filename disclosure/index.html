Disclosure

How this site operates

Transparency about funding, editorial independence, conflicts of interest, and commercial boundaries.

Editorial independence

Marijuana Fact Check is an independent editorial project by Hoyack. Evidence evaluations are produced without input from sponsors, advertisers, industry affiliates, or advocacy organizations. No external party reviews or approves claim grades before publication.

Funding

As of launch, Marijuana Fact Check is self-funded by Hoyack with no external grants, sponsorships, or advertising revenue. Future funding sources — if any — will be disclosed here before affecting any content.

Commercial boundaries

Marijuana Fact Check does not:

  • Sell, recommend, or affiliate-link to cannabis products
  • Accept payment for claim evaluations or grade assignments
  • Accept advertising from cannabis brands, dispensaries, or manufacturers
  • Provide consulting, certification, or endorsement services to cannabis companies

Future revenue hypotheses — such as sponsored research reports or paid institutional access — would be clearly labeled and editorially separated from public claim evaluations.

Conflicts of interest

Evaluators disclose relevant financial, professional, or personal interests before working on a claim. Claims with a disclosed conflict are noted on the claim record.

Corrections and complaints

See our corrections policy for how we handle errors. To raise a concern about a claim evaluation, source, or conflict of interest, contact hello@hoyack.com.

Medical and legal disclaimer

Marijuana Fact Check is not medical advice. Evidence grades describe the quality and direction of published research. They do not constitute treatment recommendations, prescribing guidance, or clinical decision support. Consult a licensed healthcare provider for any medical decision.

Marijuana Fact Check is not legal advice. Cannabis laws vary by jurisdiction. Nothing on this site constitutes legal guidance about cannabis possession, use, cultivation, or commerce.

Age restriction: This site is intended for adults 21 and over.
