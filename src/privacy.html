Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information:

  • Newsletter subscriptions: Email address only, provided voluntarily.
  • Claim submissions: The claim text, optional source URL, and optional email for follow-up.
  • Analytics: Anonymous page-view data via a privacy-focused analytics provider. No personal identifiers.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party ad networks.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Newsletter: To send you new claim reviews and research updates. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Claim submissions: To review and potentially publish a fact-check of the submitted claim. Your email is never published.
  • Analytics: To understand which claims are most read and improve our content.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Aggregated, anonymized analytics may be shared publicly (e.g., "most-viewed claims this month").

4. Data Retention

  • Newsletter subscriptions are retained until you unsubscribe.
  • Claim submissions are retained for editorial review and archived after processing.
  • Analytics data is retained in aggregate indefinitely; raw logs are deleted after 26 months.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. Contact us at the email address listed on our About page.

6. Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies. We may set a functional cookie to remember your filter preferences — this cookie contains no personal data and is not shared.

7. Third-Party Services

We use Netlify for hosting and form processing. Netlify's privacy policy applies to form submissions processed through their infrastructure. We do not embed third-party widgets, social media trackers, or ad networks.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us through the claim submission form (selecting "Privacy Inquiry" as the claim type) or via the email listed on our About page.

Medical Disclaimer: This site provides evidence ratings for informational purposes only.
