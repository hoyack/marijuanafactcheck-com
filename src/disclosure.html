Sponsorship & Revenue Disclosure

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Our Funding Model

Marijuana Fact Check is committed to editorial independence. Our evidence ratings are never influenced by sponsors, advertisers, or any commercial interest. Here is exactly how we are funded:

Current Revenue Sources

Sponsorship (Planned)

We intend to accept compliant sponsorship from organizations aligned with evidence-based cannabis education. Sponsorships will be clearly labeled and will never include product endorsements. Sponsor logos and disclaimers will appear on sponsored content. Sponsors have no editorial input, no advance review of content, and no influence over evidence ratings.

Premium Research Reports (Planned)

In-depth research reports on specific cannabis topics will be available for purchase. These reports synthesize existing public evidence — they do not contain proprietary research. Free summaries will always be available.

Newsletter Sponsorship (Planned)

Sponsored content within our email newsletter will be clearly demarcated from editorial content. Newsletter sponsors have no influence on claim reviews.

Consulting Referrals (Planned)

We may earn referral fees when connecting organizations with vetted cannabis research consultants. Referral relationships will be disclosed on relevant pages.

What We Will Not Do

  • No product endorsements. We will never recommend specific cannabis products, brands, or retailers.
  • No pay-for-ratings. Evidence grades are never for sale. Period.
  • No affiliate links for cannabis products. We do not earn commissions from cannabis sales.
  • No undisclosed sponsored content. Every financial relationship is disclosed here and on the relevant content.

Affiliate Disclosure

We may use affiliate links for books, research tools, or educational resources referenced in our content. These links are always marked and we earn a small commission at no cost to you. We only link to resources we have independently evaluated and found useful.

Current Sponsors & Partners

None at launch. This section will be updated when any sponsorship or partnership begins, with clear disclosure of the nature and scope of the relationship.

Editorial Independence Policy

No sponsor, advertiser, donor, or partner:

  • Reviews content before publication.
  • Has veto power over any claim rating.
  • Can influence the selection of claims for review.
  • Receives advance notice of upcoming content.

Our editorial team operates independently. The evidence determines the rating — nothing else.

Medical Disclaimer: This site provides evidence ratings for informational purposes only.
