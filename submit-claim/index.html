Submit a claim for evaluation

See a cannabis claim in the wild? Submit it. We prioritize claims with widespread circulation and verifiable evidence.

What we evaluate

  • Claims from news articles, social media, or public statements
  • Product marketing claims about health effects
  • Policy claims citing research evidence
  • Widely circulated claims with public health implications

What we do not evaluate

  • Personal anecdotes or individual experiences
  • Legal questions or regulatory interpretations
  • Product reviews or brand comparisons

Submissions join a public research queue. We may contact you for clarification. Submission does not guarantee evaluation. See our methodology for evaluation criteria.
