SOURCE STANDARD

Evidence before amplification

We link to the material that supports the grade, not just commentary about it.

Tier 1

Evidence syntheses

Systematic reviews, meta-analyses, evidence reports, and consensus reviews with transparent methods.

Tier 2

Public authorities

Regulators, public-health agencies, clinical guidelines, statutes, and official legal sources.

Tier 3

Primary studies

Human trials and observational studies, interpreted with design limits, sample, endpoints, and conflicts visible.

Context only

Preclinical and commentary

Animal, laboratory, press, and expert commentary can frame a question but cannot alone prove a human health claim.
