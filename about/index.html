ABOUT THE DESK

Less certainty theater. More traceable judgment.

Marijuana Fact Check is an editorial evidence database built for adults trying to separate cannabis research from sales copy, slogans, and viral claims.

EDITORIAL CHARTER

Audience and problem

Consumers, caregivers, journalists, employers, and policy teams encounter claims that collapse study design, dose, product type, population, and law into one confident sentence. We turn those sentences into reviewable records.

What we publish

Evidence records, explainers about uncertainty, source notes, correction logs, and research briefs. We do not provide individual medical advice, legal advice, dosage guidance, product rankings, or dispensary links.

How the project can earn revenue

The MVP tests a free evidence newsletter, research-report waitlist, and clearly labeled sponsor inquiries. Any future sponsorship is compliance-reviewed and separated from editorial grades. No dispensary affiliates or product calls to action are permitted in this release.

Ownership

Published by Hoyack. The content owner maintains the review queue and monthly release cadence; technical operations, analytics, monitoring, and deployment follow the public runbook in the repository.
